EbookLib

OPDS eBook Catalog Browser with iCloud Sync

About EbookLib

EbookLib is a powerful and modern iOS application that allows users to browse added OPDS eBook catalogs, browse and download eBooks in desired formats. Users can add and store their favorite catalogs in iCloud, ensuring access across all their iOS devices. Downloaded eBooks (FB2, ePub, mobi) can be read using the reader of choice (ePub Reader, Books, FBReader etc).

Key Features

  • Add, edit, delete OPDS Catalogs
  • Browse eBooks in catalogs
  • Manage catalogs stored in iCloud
  • Catalog list is shared across iPhone and iPad
  • Download eBooks in FB2, ePub, and mobi formats
  • Read downloaded eBooks with your preferred reader
  • Built-in ePub reader with shared eBook progress and bookmarks (yet to come in future releases)

Get Started

Download EbookLib from the Apple App Store and start exploring a world of free eBooks today!
