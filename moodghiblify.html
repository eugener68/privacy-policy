Your Mood, Ghibli-fied!

About MoodGhiblify

MoodGhiblify is a magical AI-powered app that turns your photo into a Studio Ghibli-style avatar that reflects your current mood. Whether you’re happy, sad, angry, or excited — just pick a photo, select how you feel, and let the Ghibli-inspired magic do the rest.

Key Features

  • Take a photo or choose one from your gallery
  • Select your current mood from a simple list
  • AI transforms your photo into a Ghibli-style portrait
  • Supports a variety of moods: happy, sad, angry, excited, and more
  • Save or share your mood avatar with friends
  • Powered by a custom-trained model for authentic animation-inspired results

Get Started

Download MoodGhiblify from the App Store and express yourself in the most enchanting way possible. It’s your face, your feelings — reimagined through the lens of magical animation.
